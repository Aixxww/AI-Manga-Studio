## 实战拼接示例

### 示例1：愤怒的职场主角

```
Vertical black and white Japanese manga page, screentone shading, high contrast line art, single manga panel, monochrome,
[角色锚点：1boy, messy black hair, dead fish eyes, heavy eyebags, tired expression, loose unbuttoned office suit, necktie]
[动作表情：is slamming his hands on a desk in frustration, screaming with a comic jagged speech bubble outline next to him]
[场景特效：messy office background, dramatic dark lighting, vertical speed lines showing intense emotion]
```

### 示例2：绝望的主角

```
Vertical black and white Japanese manga page, screentone shading, high contrast line art, single manga panel, monochrome,
[角色锚点：1boy, messy black hair, dead fish eyes, heavy eyebags, tired expression, loose unbuttoned office suit, necktie]
```

## 生成检查清单

- [ ] 画风基调是否完整（黑线/网点/单格）
- [ ] 角色视觉锚点是否正确（5-8个英文标签）
- [ ] 动作描述是否具体（不仅仅是动作名称）
- [ ] 场景描述是否详细（背景细节）
- [ ] 效果描述是否有助氛围（光影/线条）

---

## 常见错误

❌ 错误：`A man is angry`
✅ 正确：`A young man with messy hair is slamming his hands on a desk, screaming with frustration`

❌ 错误：`In a room`
✅ 正确：`In a messy cramped office with papers scattered everywhere`

❌ 错误：`Dark mood`
✅ 正确：`Dark dramatic lighting with despair screentone pattern`
